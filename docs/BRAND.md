## Lead Tracking & Billing Process

### How We Track Leads

**Form Submissions:**
- All web forms connected to your CRM
- We have database access to see submissions
- Easy to count and verify

**Phone Calls:**
- Unique Google Voice number displayed on website
- Call history available to both parties
- Clear record of incoming calls

**Monthly Reconciliation:**
1. **We send:** Detailed lead report (forms + calls from tracking number)
2. **You verify:** Cross-reference with your CRM, flag any spam/wrong numbers
3. **We bill:** Simple invoice for verified leads via QuickBooks
4. **You pay:** Monthly payment based on actual leads generated

### Why This Works
- No CRM access required from us
- You maintain control of your data
- Transparent, auditable, disputable if needed
- Easy to automate: lead count → invoice → QuickBooks
- Simple for accounting on both sides

## Operational Excellence: Lead Tracking & Billing

### Automation Strategy

To scale the lead-based model efficiently:

**Lead Counting (Automated):**
- Form submissions: Pull from website database directly
- Phone calls: Google Voice API integration
- Monthly report: Auto-generated with dates, counts, lead source

**QuickBooks Integration:**
- Calculate total leads × cost per lead = invoice amount
- Auto-generate invoice with line items (forms: X at $Y, calls: Z at $W)
- Send to QuickBooks for payment tracking
- Client receives clear, itemized invoice

**Future Scaling:**
- Build dashboard for real-time lead visibility
- SMS/email notifications when leads arrive
- CRM integrations (Zapier, direct API) for one-click lead verification
- Automated dispute resolution workflow

### Why This Matters

This operational clarity is part of our brand promise: transparency + simplicity. Clients never wonder what they're being billed for; the system shows it in real-time.
